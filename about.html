Introduction

Resunga Jal Private Limited is a proposed company registered under companies Act-2063, for production and sale of proccessed drinking water in Nepal, a venture by young experts, potential investors and other stakeholders with aim of producing high quality , affordable product which will contribute to improved drinking water and realization of direct and indirect economic benifits to the country. The company has been established with an aim to fulfill the gap of demand and supply of fresh and quatlity drinking water in Nepal.The project is based on Resuga Municipality,ward no.3, Tamghas,Gulmi District.The location of the project has been stragically chosen with thd proper study of the vaibility of the project and the technical analysis of the resources. This project is startup by young , dynamic , and highly energetic entrepreneurs. The project is commited on supplying quality drinking water with high focus on health, safety, and price. Resunga Jal Pvt. Ltd. is a leading producer of high-quality bottled water, offering a range of sizes including 500ml,1000ml and 20000ml bottles. With a state-of-art- semi-automatic plant,the company combines the best technological advancement with a commitment to excellence.
