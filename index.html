Our Company

The situation in Nepal regarding the scarcity of safer tap water and the increasing demand for alternative solutions such as purified bottled water. It is evident that bottled water has gained popularity due to the trust placed by people in its safer water quality.
Resunga Jal Pvt. Ltd. is one of the first bottled water company of Gulmi District situated at Resunga Municipality -03.Considering the technical aspects of the bottled water industry in Nepal, our company aims to address these challenges and capitalize on the opportunities presented.
By prioritizing the production of safe and high-quality drinking water, implementing advance technology semi-automatic plant, adhering to regulatory standards set by the Department of Food Technology and Quality Control, and leveraging the purity and demand for hill-sourced water, our company can position itself as a leading provider of safe and cost-effective processed bottled drinking water within the Gulmi District markets.
Our company utilizes advance equipment and processess to deliver high quality and cost-effective processed bottled water of 1L ,0.5L and 20L capacities.It is worth noting that bottles with 1L capacity or below(i.e, 0.5L) are usually automatically filled and pakaged, whereas the filling process for 20L Jars is often semi-automatic or manual.
